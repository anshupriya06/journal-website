Journal of Ancient Science,
Education & Research

Welcome to the International Journal of Ancient Sciences, Education, and Research (IJASER), a peer-reviewed, open-access platform dedicated to the exploration and dissemination of knowledge related to ancient sciences and their relevance in modern education and research.

At IJASER, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and contemporary scholarship by publishing high-quality research on ancient knowledge systems, including Vedic sciences, Ayurveda, astronomy, mathematics, philosophy, architecture, and more. Our journal fosters interdisciplinary studies and encourages dialogue between ancient practices and modern scientific advancements.

We are committed to advancing educational perspectives by showcasing scholarly articles that contribute to the understanding, teaching, and application of ancient sciences in the current era. IJASER serves as a global forum for educators, researchers, and practitioners to share insights, exchange ideas, and contribute to the growing body of knowledge in these fields.

Join us in preserving the rich heritage of ancient sciences while promoting their integration into educational and research practices for the benefit of future generations.
